I’d like to spot a thing. In your solution you suggest to fill the text of the elements within the template with:

var entry = template.clone().removeAttr(“id”);
entry.find(”.title”).text(item.title);

I’d prefer to do like this, just a more compact way to write the same thing.

var entry = template.clone().removeAttr(“id”);
$(”.title”, entry).text(item.title);

What do you think?
