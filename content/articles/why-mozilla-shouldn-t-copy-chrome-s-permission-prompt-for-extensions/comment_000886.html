The Chrome team published a paper about their work on fighting malicious extensions in the Chrome Web Store – http://static.googleusercontent.com/media/research.google.com/en//pubs/archive/43824.pdf. One strategy discussed towards the end of the paper is to monitor the behavior of extensions in the wild (rather than as part of the review process) and to flag suspicious behavior for human review.
